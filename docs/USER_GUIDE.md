## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit [artisanlink.co.ke](https://artisanlink.co.ke) (or your deployed URL)
2. Click "Sign Up" in the header
3. Choose your sign-up method:
   - Email and password
   - Google account
   - Phone number
4. Verify your email or phone number
5. Complete your profile

### Choosing Your Role

After signing up, you'll be directed to complete your profile. By default, all users start as **Clients**. If you're a skilled artisan, you can request to become an artisan through your profile settings.

## For Clients

### Finding Artisans

#### Search by Profession

1. Go to the **Find Artisans** page
2. Use the search bar to enter a profession (e.g., "carpenter", "plumber")
3. Results show artisans matching your search

#### Filter Results

Narrow down results using filters:

- **Location** - Filter by county or city
- **Rating** - Minimum star rating
- **Price Range** - Hourly rate range
- **Availability** - Only show available artisans
- **Verified** - Only show verified artisans
- **Specialization** - Specific skills

#### Sort Results

Sort artisans by:
- **Rating** - Highest rated first
- **Reviews** - Most reviewed first
- **Rate** - By hourly rate
- **Distance** - Nearest first (requires location)
- **Recent** - Newest members first

### Viewing Artisan Profiles

Click on an artisan card to view their full profile:

- **Bio** - About the artisan
- **Portfolio** - Past work with photos
- **Reviews** - Client feedback
- **Specializations** - Skills and expertise
- **Location** - Service area
- **Availability** - Current availability status

### Contacting Artisans

1. From an artisan's profile, click **"Contact"** or **"Send Message"**
2. If this is your first message, a new conversation is created
3. Write your inquiry (describe your project, timeline, etc.)
4. Send the message

**Tips for good first messages:**
- Describe your project clearly
- Include your location
- Mention your timeline
- Ask about availability

### Saving Favorite Artisans

1. On any artisan profile, click the **heart icon** or **"Save"**
2. View saved artisans from your dashboard under **"Saved Artisans"**
3. Remove by clicking the icon again

### Leaving Reviews

After working with an artisan:

1. Go to your **Messages** and find the conversation
2. Click **"Leave Review"** or go to the artisan's profile
3. Rate from 1-5 stars
4. Write your feedback (optional but recommended)
5. Add project details (optional):
   - Project title
   - Approximate cost
6. Submit your review

**Note**: Reviews require admin approval before appearing publicly.

### Managing Conversations

Your **Messages** dashboard shows all conversations:

- **Active** - Ongoing conversations
- **Archived** - Past conversations
- Unread messages show a notification badge
- Click a conversation to view and reply

## For Artisans

### Becoming an Artisan

1. Go to your **Profile Settings**
2. Click **"Become an Artisan"**
3. Fill in your professional details:
   - Profession/Trade
   - Years of experience
   - Service area (county, city)
   - Hourly rate
   - Bio describing your work
4. Upload profile photo
5. Submit for verification

### Verification Process

After applying:

1. Your application is reviewed by administrators
2. You may be asked to provide:
   - ID verification
   - Proof of skills (certificates, photos of work)
   - Business registration (if applicable)
3. Once verified, you'll receive a notification
4. The "Verified" badge appears on your profile

### Managing Your Portfolio

Showcase your best work:

#### Adding Portfolio Items

1. Go to **Artisan Dashboard** → **Portfolio**
2. Click **"Add Project"**
3. Fill in details:
   - Title
   - Description
   - Category
   - Tags
   - Duration
   - Cost (optional)
4. Upload photos (main image required, additional images optional)
5. Set visibility (public/private)
6. Mark as featured (shows prominently)
7. Save

#### Editing/Removing Items

- Click **Edit** on any portfolio item to update
- Click **Delete** to remove (cannot be undone)

### Setting Your Availability

Let clients know when you're available:

1. Go to **Artisan Dashboard** → **Settings**
2. Toggle **"Available for Work"**
3. When unavailable, you won't appear in search results (optional setting)

### Managing Specializations

Add your specific skills:

1. Go to **Profile** → **Specializations**
2. Click **"Add Specialization"**
3. Enter:
   - Skill name
   - Skill level (Beginner, Intermediate, Expert)
   - Years of experience
   - Description
4. Save

### Subscription Plans

Access premium features with a subscription:

#### Free Features
- Basic profile
- Limited portfolio items
- Receive messages

#### Premium Features (Subscription Required)
- Featured placement in search
- Unlimited portfolio items
- Priority in search results
- Analytics dashboard
- Verified badge eligibility

#### Subscribing

1. Go to **Artisan Dashboard** → **Subscription**
2. Choose a plan:
   - **Monthly** - KES 500/month
   - **Annual** - KES 5,000/year (save 17%)
3. Enter your M-Pesa phone number
4. Click **"Subscribe"**
5. Enter your M-Pesa PIN when prompted on your phone
6. Subscription activates immediately upon payment

### Responding to Clients

Build good relationships:

1. Check your **Messages** regularly
2. Respond promptly (within 24 hours recommended)
3. Be professional and clear about:
   - Pricing
   - Timeline
   - What's included
   - Terms of work
4. Move conversations to phone/WhatsApp for quick coordination

### Viewing Your Stats

Track your performance:

- **Profile Views** - How many people viewed your profile
- **Messages Received** - Client inquiries
- **Reviews** - Your ratings and feedback
- **Search Appearances** - How often you appear in search

## For Administrators

### Accessing Admin Dashboard

1. Sign in with an admin account
2. Navigate to `/admin-dashboard` or click **"Admin"** in the menu
3. View the admin overview with key metrics

### User Management

#### Viewing Users

1. Go to **Admin** → **Users**
2. See all registered users
3. Filter by:
   - Role (Client, Artisan, Admin)
   - Status (Active, Suspended)
   - Registration date

#### Managing User Accounts

- **View Details** - Click a user to see full profile
- **Suspend** - Temporarily disable an account
- **Activate** - Re-enable a suspended account
- **Change Role** - Promote/demote users

### Artisan Verification

#### Reviewing Applications

1. Go to **Admin** → **Verifications**
2. See pending verification requests
3. Click to review an application
4. View submitted documents and profile

#### Processing Verifications

- **Approve** - Grant verified status
- **Reject** - Deny with reason
- Add notes for internal reference

### Content Moderation

#### Review Moderation

1. Go to **Admin** → **Moderation**
2. See pending reviews
3. Review content for:
   - Inappropriate language
   - Fake reviews
   - Policy violations
4. **Approve** or **Reject** each review

### Reports & Analytics

#### Viewing Reports

1. Go to **Admin** → **Reports**
2. Choose report type:
   - User statistics
   - Revenue reports
   - Activity reports
3. Select date range
4. Export as CSV or PDF

#### Analytics Dashboard

View key metrics:
- Total users (with growth trends)
- Active artisans
- Subscription revenue
- Popular professions
- Geographic distribution

### System Monitoring

1. Go to **Admin** → **System**
2. View:
   - System health status
   - Database statistics
   - API response times
   - Error rates
   - Recent activity logs

### Managing Settings

1. Go to **Admin** → **Settings**
2. Configure:
   - Platform settings
   - Feature toggles
   - Email templates
   - Payment settings

## FAQ

### General

**Q: Is ArtisanLink free to use?**

A: Yes! Creating an account and browsing artisans is free for clients. Artisans have a free tier with basic features and optional premium subscriptions.

**Q: How do I report a problem?**

A: Contact us through the **"Help"** section or email support@artisanlink.co.ke.

**Q: Is my information secure?**

A: Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and never share your personal data without consent.

### For Clients

**Q: How do I know if an artisan is trustworthy?**

A: Look for:
- Verified badge (identity confirmed)
- High ratings and reviews
- Complete profile with portfolio
- Responsive communication

**Q: What if I have an issue with an artisan?**

A: 1) Try to resolve directly with the artisan. 2) If unresolved, contact our support team. 3) Leave an honest review about your experience.

**Q: Can I hire artisans outside my area?**

A: Yes, but check if the artisan services your location. Many artisans note their service area in their profile.

### For Artisans

**Q: How do I get more clients?**

A: 
- Complete your profile fully
- Add portfolio items with quality photos
- Respond to messages quickly
- Ask happy clients for reviews
- Stay verified and available

**Q: What happens if my subscription expires?**

A: Your account reverts to free features. Your profile and portfolio remain, but premium features are disabled.

**Q: How do I get the verified badge?**

A: Apply through your dashboard. Verification requires identity confirmation and may include document review.

**Q: How do payments work?**

A: Payments for work are handled directly between you and clients. ArtisanLink only processes subscription payments.

### Technical

**Q: Why can't I log in?**

A: Try:
1. Check your email/password
2. Use "Forgot Password" to reset
3. Clear browser cookies
4. Try a different browser
5. Contact support if issues persist

**Q: The site is slow. What can I do?**

A: Try:
1. Refresh the page
2. Check your internet connection
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try a different browser

**Q: How do I delete my account?**

A: Go to **Settings** → **Account** → **Delete Account**. This action is permanent.
